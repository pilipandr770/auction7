Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2025

1. General Provisions

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting users' personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR, Regulation (EU) 2016/679) and other European Union laws.

2. What Data We Collect

  • First name, last name, email address, phone number
  • Login data (username, password)
  • Payment information (if transactions are made)
  • IP address, browser data, cookies
  • Other information you voluntarily provide

3. Purpose of Data Processing

  • User registration and identification
  • Providing access to the service and its features
  • Processing payments and ensuring transaction security
  • Improving service quality, analytics, and statistics
  • Fulfilling legal obligations
  • Contacting the user (email, messages)

4. Data Storage and Protection

Your data is stored on secure servers within the EU. We use modern technical and organizational measures to protect data from unauthorized access, loss, or destruction.

5. Data Transfer to Third Parties

We do not transfer your personal data to third parties, except as required by law or necessary to provide the service (e.g., to payment providers).

6. Your Rights

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to correct inaccuracies
  • Right to delete data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

7. Cookies

We use cookies to improve the website, analytics, and personalization. You can manage cookie settings in your browser.

8. Changes to the Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The current version is always available on our website.
