How to participate in auctions profitably and responsibly

🎯 Fair price

In our auction system, you have a real chance to buy goods cheaper. Only verified sellers who comply with EU rules are allowed. The auction starts at the market price, which can be checked by the seller via Check24. With each new participant, the price drops and the funds go to escrow. During the auction, you will have only 5 seconds to decide — take the item or wait.

🚪 Entry price and the importance of choice

The entry price is set by the seller. It never exceeds 1% of the item's value and always remains affordable. All payments go to escrow and are transferred to the seller only after the buyer confirms receipt of the item.

💡 Buyer benefits

You can immediately buy the item by paying the entry price and clicking "Take". This is the fastest and guaranteed way. Or you can wait for the price to drop and view it using the price view function (with a discount for token holders).
